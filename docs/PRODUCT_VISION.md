# RepairGraph

## Product Vision v1.0

### Why We Exist

Collision repair has become one of the most technically complex industries in manufacturing.

Modern vehicles are built from dozens of material types, hundreds of OEM procedures, advanced driver assistance systems, high-voltage electrical systems, and increasingly strict repair requirements. The information required to repair a vehicle safely already exists, but it exists as thousands of pages of disconnected documents.

Technicians, estimators, and managers are expected to transform static documentation into a safe, efficient repair process.

That translation happens almost entirely inside people's heads.

RepairGraph exists to eliminate that gap.

Our mission is to convert procedural documentation into operational intelligence.

---

## The Problem

Today's repair information systems answer questions like:

- Where is the procedure?
- What does the OEM say?
- Which page contains the specification?

Those are documentation problems.

Repair shops do not struggle because documentation is unavailable.

They struggle because documentation is not operational.

Every repair requires people to mentally answer questions such as:

- What actually needs to happen?
- What depends on what?
- What can begin today?
- What is blocked?
- What has been completed?
- What evidence is still missing?
- Are we still compliant with OEM requirements?

Today's software rarely answers those questions.

---

## Our Belief

We believe documentation should not be read.

It should be understood.

Every procedural document already contains the knowledge required to build an operational model of the work.

Computers should perform that translation automatically.

People should spend their time making decisions, not reconstructing workflows.

---

## What RepairGraph Is

RepairGraph is a procedural intelligence platform.

It transforms OEM repair documentation into a living operational model of a repair.

Rather than treating procedures as isolated documents, RepairGraph identifies relationships between operations, dependencies, materials, inspections, quality gates, calibrations, corrosion protection, and workflow state.

The result is not a better document viewer.

The result is a digital representation of how the repair actually happens.

---

## What RepairGraph Does

RepairGraph reviews a repair before work begins.

It identifies:

- procedural dependencies
- workflow phases
- quality assurance requirements
- required inspections
- document completeness
- operational blockers
- compliance risks
- recommended next actions

Instead of asking users to interpret documentation, RepairGraph presents only the information that matters.

---

## The Customer

RepairGraph is designed primarily for the people responsible for successful repairs.

This includes:

- Collision center managers
- Production managers
- Blueprint specialists
- OEM compliance teams
- Multi-shop operators (MSOs)
- Dealer collision centers

Technicians remain critical users, but they are not the primary customer.

RepairGraph exists to improve operational decision-making across the repair process.

---

## The Core Product Promise

When a repair enters the shop, RepairGraph should answer four questions immediately:

1. What matters?
2. What is missing?
3. What is blocked?
4. What should happen next?

Every feature we build should strengthen one or more of those answers.

---

## Our Design Philosophy

RepairGraph should reduce cognitive load.

Users should never feel overwhelmed by documentation.

Instead of exposing every available piece of information, RepairGraph should prioritize the information requiring action.

The product should behave like an experienced production manager reviewing every repair before technicians begin work.

---

## What RepairGraph Is Not

RepairGraph is not:

- an estimating platform
- a document storage system
- a PDF viewer
- an OEM database
- a replacement for ALLDATA, CCC, Mitchell, or OEM portals

Those systems manage information.

RepairGraph manages operational understanding.

---

## Why This Matters

Collision repair continues to become more complex every year.

More advanced materials.

More electronics.

More calibrations.

More OEM procedures.

More opportunities for expensive mistakes.

The industry's challenge is no longer access to information.

The challenge is converting information into execution.

RepairGraph exists to bridge that gap.

---

## Our Long-Term Vision

The architecture behind RepairGraph is intentionally broader than collision repair.

At its core, RepairGraph transforms procedural documentation into operational intelligence.

That pattern applies anywhere complex work is governed by structured procedures.

Potential future applications include:

- aviation maintenance
- heavy equipment service
- industrial manufacturing
- energy infrastructure
- medical equipment maintenance
- regulatory inspection workflows

Collision repair is where we begin because it is a domain we understand deeply.

It is not necessarily where the platform ends.

---

## The Product Standard

Every new feature should make the answer to one question easier:

> Can someone make a better operational decision because RepairGraph exists?

If the answer is no, we should reconsider building it.

Features are not the product.

Operational understanding is the product.

---

## The Company We Want to Build

We are not building software that stores procedures.

We are building software that understands them.

We are not building better documents.

We are building better decisions.

And ultimately, we believe that every complex procedure, regardless of industry, should exist not as static documentation, but as an operational model that computers can reason about and people can act upon.

---

## Company Sentence

> RepairGraph reviews every repair before work begins, identifies what matters, and tells the shop exactly what needs attention next.

This is the sentence we can build the company around.

## Strategic Note

The graph is not the product.

The graph is the implementation.

The real product is decision intelligence for procedural work.
