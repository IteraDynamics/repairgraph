# RepairGraph Data Handling Policy

## Purpose

RepairGraph is designed to operate on customer-authorized repair documentation.

The system is intended to transform OEM repair procedures into structured operational intelligence.

RepairGraph is not intended to redistribute OEM documentation or bypass OEM licensing systems.

---

# Authorized Source Material

Examples of authorized source material may include:

- OEM repair procedures
- construction/material diagrams
- weld symbol definitions
- corrosion protection procedures
- calibration procedures
- customer-provided repair documentation

Users are responsible for ensuring they possess the appropriate authorization or subscription rights to access source material.

---

# Transformation Philosophy

RepairGraph focuses on:

- semantic extraction
- procedural normalization
- structural relationship mapping
- graph construction
- contextual operational intelligence

The platform objective is to transform procedural information into machine-readable operational structures.

---

# Storage Philosophy

RepairGraph should minimize unnecessary redistribution or exposure of raw OEM documents.

Long-term emphasis should prioritize:

- structured graph representations
- normalized repair intelligence
- procedural metadata
- operational relationships

rather than persistent redistribution of source PDFs.

---

# Future Direction

Future RepairGraph deployments may support:

- customer-hosted ingestion
- local document processing
- enterprise-controlled repair libraries
- role-based access controls
- OEM-compliant workflow integrations
