# RepairGraph Product Thesis

## Core Thesis

Physical-world repair workflows are becoming too complex for static document systems.

Modern collision repair increasingly depends on:

- advanced material engineering
- mixed joining methods
- structural repair constraints
- calibration dependencies
- corrosion protection requirements
- vehicle-specific topology
- procedural sequencing
- hidden structural relationships

OEM repair procedures currently exist primarily as human-readable documentation.

RepairGraph is built on the belief that these procedures can instead become machine-readable operational intelligence.

## Strategic Direction

RepairGraph converts authorized OEM repair documentation into:

- structured repair graphs
- procedural topology models
- material intelligence layers
- joining method taxonomies
- dependency graphs
- contextual workflow intelligence

The long-term direction is not simply document retrieval.

The long-term direction is contextual procedural intelligence for physical-world workflows.

## Why Collision Repair

Collision repair is an unusually strong proving ground because it contains:

- highly procedural workflows
- visual and spatial operations
- expensive mistakes
- increasing structural complexity
- growing ADAS/calibration dependencies
- weak software incumbents
- fragmented operational intelligence

## Long-Term Vision

RepairGraph aims to become a contextual intelligence layer capable of supporting:

- operational copilots
- repair QA systems
- supplement inference
- technician workflow guidance
- spatial overlays
- wearable/AR repair systems
- structural dependency analysis
- procedural reasoning systems

## Initial Focus

The initial focus is intentionally narrow:

- Honda body repair procedures
- rear side outer panel / quarter panel replacement
- roof and side panel construction
- weld symbol normalization
- corrosion protection requirements

The purpose of this narrow scope is to discover the canonical primitives required to represent collision repair as a structured graph system.
